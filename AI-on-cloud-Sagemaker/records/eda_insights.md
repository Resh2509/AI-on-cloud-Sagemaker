# Customer Churn EDA Findings

## Dataset Overview

- Rows: 7043
- Columns: 20
- Target: Churn

## Key Findings

### 1. Tenure

Customers with low tenure churn more frequently.

Long-term customers are more likely to stay.

### 2. Monthly Charges

Customers with higher monthly charges have a higher tendency to churn.

### 3. Contract Type

Month-to-month contracts show the highest churn.

One-year and two-year contracts show significantly lower churn.

### 4. Class Distribution

Approximately 26% of customers churned.

The dataset is moderately imbalanced.
